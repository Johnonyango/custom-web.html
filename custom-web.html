Poodle Pet

Poodles are clean animals.

Some people confuse poodles with ordinary dogs.

Poodles are very clean and intelligent

They can sit on couches by humans and they can be sent for errands like delivering a letter.

What poodles love

Poodles love the following:

  • Attention
  • Enough drinking water
  • Work

The list above suggests that poodles loves to be taken care of and to be pampered but they are not lazy!
